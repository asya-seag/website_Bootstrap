Welcome to Muddy Paws!

Hello my name is Anna, I have opened this business in 2020 and it has been so successful that now we have grown to a team of two with my assistant Tina. We are running our operations in Monifieth and Broughty Ferry area. We get really busy during holiday season and can only have 4 dogs boarding at any given time, so please do get in touch. We do deal with senior and nervous dogs and have a lot of experience with dealing with extra needs (helping dogs take medication, limited mobility etc).

Our social walks are more like a local dog club, as it is nice and easy for new dogs to get introduced to regulars from our dog walks. We highly recommend joining them if you are not sure how your dog might feel about group walks. They are very affordable, include 45 minutes in dog park and a few treats.

Our business is very transparent, we have grown mostly through private recommendations so you might have already heard about us. But if you are ever interested in reading more customer reviews, there are plenty on our facebook page: see reviews here!
